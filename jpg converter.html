Why Image Format Conversion Matters

Different image formats serve different purposes. JPEG is great for photographs with small file sizes, PNG supports transparency, WEBP offers superior compression, HEIC is common on Apple devices, and SVG is perfect for logos and graphics that need to scale. Using the right format can:

  • Improve your website loading speed
  • Reduce storage space requirements
  • Maintain better image quality
  • Ensure compatibility across devices and platforms
